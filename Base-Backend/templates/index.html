Empowering Journeys, One Exam at a Time.

FindMeWriter is an accessibility-first platform that seamlessly connects visually impaired students with trusted, verified volunteer writers (scribes) for academic examinations.

Platform Features

  • For Students

    Easily post exam details and get paired with qualified scribes matching your language and location.

  • For Volunteers

    Browse exam request feeds, pick roles matching your availability, and make a lasting impact.

  • For Colleges

    Verify credentials, track scribe attendance, and ensure a secure, tamper-evident exam environment.

Why Choose FindMeWriter?

Accessibility-First

Fully WCAG 2.1 AA compliant. Designed from the ground up to support keyboard navigation, high-contrast layouts, and screen readers.

Secured & Encrypted

Identity verifications are backed by encrypted Aadhaar storage, zero-concatenation database transactions, and a tamper-evident audit log chain.

Active Backup Scribes

Never face exam day alone. Our robust matching workflow supports backup volunteer sign-ups with auto-promotion in case of emergencies.
