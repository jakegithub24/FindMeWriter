Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

1. Data Collection and Usage

We collect personal information necessary to connect students with volunteer writers. This includes name, email address, phone number, role, and Aadhaar card photocopies (for students and volunteers).

2. Security and Encryption

We prioritize security. Aadhaar numbers are stored as one-way SHA-256 hashes, and Aadhaar photocopies are encrypted using AES symmetric encryption (Fernet) before storage on our server disks. These files are served only to authorized institutional administrators for verification purposes.

3. Data Retention

Aadhaar copies are retained only as long as the user account is active, plus one year for archival audit requirements. Users may request logical deletion of their accounts at any time.
